Build a fully functional bilingual (Arabic first, English available) fitness coaching web app demo.

Constraints:
- No external services/APIs.
- Do not hotlink images. Include coach and workout illustrations locally under public/images/.
- Persist user profile, workouts, and progress in browser storage.

Features:
- Onboarding (demo): goals, experience, available equipment, weekly schedule.
- Dashboard: today's workout, weekly calendar, streaks, and progress highlights.
- Workouts library: search + filters (difficulty, duration, equipment), and workout detail pages.
- Workout player: timer, sets/reps checklist, rest timer, and "mark complete".
- Progress: track body measurements (demo), personal bests (demo), and completion history.
- Meal ideas (demo): meal cards with macros and shopping list generator (local).
- Settings/help: language toggle and "Reset demo data".

Content and localization:
- Seed at least 18 workouts with Arabic-first names/instructions + good English translations.
- Default Arabic; RTL/LTR switching.

UX quality:
- Responsive, accessible, and clear empty/loading states.
