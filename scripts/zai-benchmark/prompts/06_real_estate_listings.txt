Build a fully functional bilingual (Arabic first, English available) real estate listings website demo.

Constraints:
- No external services/APIs. Use seeded local data.
- Do not hotlink images. Include listing photos locally under public/images/.
- Persist saved listings and comparison list in browser storage.

Features:
- Home: featured neighborhoods, featured listings, and a quick search widget.
- Listings: search + filters (city, neighborhood, price range, bedrooms, bathrooms, property type, amenities).
- Listing details: photo gallery, key facts, floor plan image (local, can be schematic), amenities, nearby highlights (demo), and a contact form (demo).
- Map view (demo, offline): show listings on a simple illustrated map image with clickable pins and a list synced to selection.
- Save/favorites: save listings and manage them on a Saved page.
- Compare: compare up to 3 listings side-by-side.
- Agents: directory of agents with profiles and contact forms (demo).

Content and localization:
- Seed at least 20 listings across 3 cities and multiple neighborhoods with realistic details and pricing.
- Arabic-first content + good English translations; default Arabic with RTL/LTR switching.

UX quality:
- Clear navigation, empty/loading states, and a settings/help page with "Reset demo data".
