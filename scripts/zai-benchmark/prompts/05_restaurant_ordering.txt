Build a fully functional bilingual (Arabic first, English available) restaurant ordering website demo.

Constraints:
- No external services/APIs.
- Do not hotlink images. Add menu item images locally under public/images/.
- Persist cart and past orders in browser storage.

Features:
- Home: restaurant story, hours/location (demo), popular dishes, and current offers.
- Menu: categories (starters, mains, sides, desserts, drinks), search, dietary filters (vegan, gluten-free, spicy).
- Item details: photo, description, allergens (demo), and customization options (size, spice level, add-ons).
- Cart: quantity updates, notes per item, pickup vs delivery (demo), scheduled time selector (demo).
- Checkout: customer details and address (if delivery), order review, place order.
- Order tracking: timeline view (received, preparing, ready, out for delivery, delivered) with realistic timers (demo).
- Orders history: list past orders and reorder.

Content and localization:
- Seed at least 35 menu items with Arabic-first names/descriptions + good English translations.
- Default language Arabic; RTL/LTR switching.
- Include local images for menu items and category banners.

UX quality:
- Great empty states, loading states, and helpful validation messages.
- Help page with "Reset demo data".
